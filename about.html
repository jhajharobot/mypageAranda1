ABOUT | FOODPANDA

The Filipino Cuisine Restaurant, formerly known as “Tara kain tayo” started as a humble eatery along the streets of Cebu. But with Filipino Cuisine undeniably delicious dishes, mouthwatering words of recommendation quickly spread into every Cebuanos’ palate. With that , Filipino Cuisine instantly became one of the well-loved restaurants in Cebu. Today, Filipino Cuisine to satisfy every Filipino’s appetite nationwide. Using only the freshest ingredients available, Filipino Cuisine cooks up a storm of delicious Pinoy food in every corner of the Philippines. And the creator of Filipino Cuisine is Mr. Jhamire Lei Aranda a Professional Chef in the Philippines.
